# Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation

- Repository quality gates, configuration, logging, health checks, PostgreSQL, object storage, migrations, and CI.
- Telegram token rotation and secret-management setup.
- Allowlist administration and private-chat enforcement.

## Phase 1 — Assisted image MVP

- Durable Telegram conversation.
- Nano Banana, ChatGPT, and Claude prompt-assistant adapters.
- Nano Banana and OpenAI image-renderer adapters.
- Adaptive creative brief and clarification flow.
- Review, explicit confirmation, queue, generation, delivery, history, and cancellation.
- Quotas, budgets, auditing, retries, reconciliation, and cleanup.
- Future-publishing notice after delivery.

## Phase 2 — Image iteration

- Reference-image upload and validation.
- Variations and edits using previous assets.
- Saved brand constraints and reusable templates with explicit privacy controls.
- Provider quality evaluation and routing policy.

## Phase 3 — Video generation

- Separate video domain specification and provider capability registry.
- Long-running operation monitoring and stricter cost controls.
- Video-specific storage and delivery policies.

## Phase 4 — Social publishing

- New ADRs and threat model before implementation.
- Social accounts, OAuth credentials, approvals, schedules, platform variants, publishing attempts, and analytics.
- Independent permissions and operational boundary from generation.
